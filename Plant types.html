What are plants?

plants are living things. The Habitat on land and on water the important thing about plants is that they can thrive anywhere, from snowclad mountians to the dry dust of the desert, Plants can be found everywhere. Plants can be divided into groups: Flowering Plants and Non flowering Plants.

  • Flowering Plants Plants which bare flowers are flowering plants. Examples are Orchids, Roses, Sunflowers, etc.
  • Non Flowering Plants: plants do not bare flowers are called non flowering plants, Examples are ferns, moses. ect etc.
